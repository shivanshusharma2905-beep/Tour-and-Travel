Our Tour Packages

At SS Tours & Travels, we believe that every traveler deserves a journey as unique as their dreams. That’s why we’ve carefully designed a variety of tour packages to suit every type of explorer — from adventure seekers and honeymoon couples to families and corporate travelers. Our goal is to make your trip seamless, memorable, and full of discovery.

Our Domestic Tour Packages allow you to uncover the hidden gems of India — from the snow-capped peaks of Himachal Pradesh, the royal palaces of Rajasthan, the serene backwaters of Kerala, to the golden beaches of Goa. Each itinerary is designed to give you a perfect mix of culture, adventure, relaxation, and local cuisine. Whether it’s a weekend getaway or a long family holiday, our team ensures every detail is handled with care.

For those who dream beyond borders, our International Tour Packages bring the world closer to you. Explore the romantic streets of Paris, the historical wonders of Egypt, the modern skyline of Dubai, or the breathtaking landscapes of Switzerland. Our experienced travel planners create customized itineraries, arrange visa assistance, flight bookings, hotel reservations, and local transfers — making your international trip smooth and stress-free.

We also specialize in Honeymoon Packages crafted to create unforgettable moments for newlyweds. Picture yourself watching the sunset on a private beach, enjoying candlelight dinners under the stars, or taking scenic mountain drives — we make sure your first journey together as a couple becomes a cherished memory for life. Our romantic destinations include Maldives, Bali, Kashmir, Shimla, and Santorini among many others.

For thrill seekers, our Adventure Packages are packed with excitement and adrenaline. Go river rafting in Rishikesh, paragliding in Manali, desert camping in Jaisalmer, or trekking through the Himalayan trails. Every adventure package includes safety measures, professional guidance, and all essential amenities to ensure an unforgettable experience.

Corporate clients can take advantage of our Business and Group Tour Packages that are ideal for team outings, incentive trips, and conferences. We take care of travel logistics, accommodation, event planning, and entertainment activities — so you can focus on building relationships while we handle the rest.
